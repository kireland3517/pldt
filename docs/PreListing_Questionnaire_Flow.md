# The Questionnaire — Real Branching Flow

This shows the actual parent/child flow. A presence question (▶) reveals its condition questions ONLY when the answer establishes that part of the home exists. If you don't have a deck, no deck questions appear — there is no "only if you have one" text anywhere, because the question simply isn't shown.

Indented questions (└) appear only when the parent answer reveals them.


---

## Step 1 · About your home + its condition

*Each top-level question establishes whether you have something. If you do, its follow-ups appear right under it. If you don't, they never show.*


▶ **Roughly how old is the roof, and is it the original roof?**
   _Answer:_ 0-5 yrs  ·  6-15 yrs  ·  15+ yrs  ·  unsure
   _If this confirms you have one, these appear:_
      └ Any roof leaks, water stains on ceilings, or missing/curling shingles?
          _Answer:_ no issues  ·  minor/cosmetic  ·  active leak or stains  ·  unsure  ("unsure" → we suggest a closer look, no cost guessed)

▶ **What does the home sit on?**
   _Answer:_ crawlspace  ·  basement  ·  slab  ·  unsure
   _If this confirms you have one, these appear:_
      └ In the crawlspace or basement, is there standing water, dampness, a musty smell, or visible cracks?
          _Answer:_ dry, no issues  ·  some dampness/smell  ·  standing water or cracks  ·  cannot access / unsure  ("cannot access / unsure" → we suggest a closer look, no cost guessed)

▶ **Is there a deck, raised porch, or balcony?**
   _Answer:_ Yes  /  No
   _If this confirms you have one, these appear:_
      └ On the deck, are any boards soft or rotted, railings loose, or gaps between stair risers?
          _Answer:_ solid  ·  cosmetic wear  ·  soft boards / loose rail / open risers  ·  unsure  ("unsure" → we suggest a closer look, no cost guessed)

▶ **Is there a front porch or exterior stairs with railings?**
   _Answer:_ Yes  /  No
   _If this confirms you have one, these appear:_
      └ On porch or exterior stairs, are handrails loose or missing, or steps uneven?
          _Answer:_ secure  ·  minor  ·  loose/missing handrail or uneven steps

▶ **Is there a garage with a powered door?**
   _Answer:_ Yes  /  No
   _If this confirms you have one, these appear:_
      └ Does the garage door open and close normally on its own power?
          _Answer:_ works normally  ·  works but damaged  ·  does not work

▶ **Is there a fireplace?**
   _Answer:_ Yes  /  No
   _If this confirms you have one, these appear:_
      └ Does the fireplace work, and has the chimney/flue been serviced?
          _Answer:_ works, serviced  ·  works, not recently serviced  ·  does not work / unsure  ("does not work / unsure" → we suggest a closer look, no cost guessed)

▶ **Roughly how old is the heating and cooling system?**
   _Answer:_ 0-5 yrs  ·  6-12 yrs  ·  12+ yrs  ·  unsure
   _If this confirms you have one, these appear:_
      └ Does the heating and cooling both run and keep the home comfortable?
          _Answer:_ both work well  ·  weak/uneven  ·  one or both not working  ·  unsure  ("unsure" → we suggest a closer look, no cost guessed)

▶ **Roughly how old is the water heater?**
   _Answer:_ 0-5 yrs  ·  6-12 yrs  ·  12+ yrs  ·  unsure
   _(no follow-ups)_

▶ **How many bathrooms, and which is the primary?**
   _Answer:_ 1  ·  2  ·  3+
   _If this confirms you have one, these appear:_
      └ Are the bathrooms dated, or is anything loose, leaking, or pulling away from the wall (vanity, toilet, tile)?
          _Answer:_ fine  ·  dated  ·  loose/leaking/separating
      └ Bathrooms: are the faucets, lighting, mirror, and hardware dated or builder-basic?
          _Answer:_ modern/updated  ·  basic but fine  ·  dated/builder-grade

▶ **Is the home on public water and sewer, or a well and/or septic system?**
   _Answer:_ public water + sewer  ·  well  ·  septic  ·  well + septic  ·  unsure
   _If this confirms you have one, these appear:_
      └ Any active leaks, low water pressure, slow drains, or a water heater that leaks or runs out fast?
          _Answer:_ no issues  ·  minor  ·  active leak / no hot water  ·  unsure  ("unsure" → we suggest a closer look, no cost guessed)

## Always asked (apply to every home)


▶ **Is the siding damaged, rotted, or showing gaps where water could get in?**
   _Answer:_ good  ·  cosmetic wear  ·  damage/rot  ·  unsure  ("unsure" → we suggest a closer look, no cost guessed)

▶ **Near the panel or in the attic, do you see exposed wires, open boxes without covers, or missing outlet/switch cover plates? (Visible signs only.)**
   _Answer:_ none visible  ·  some missing covers  ·  exposed wires / open boxes  ·  cannot access / unsure  ("cannot access / unsure" → we suggest a closer look, no cost guessed)

▶ **Are there working smoke detectors, and a carbon monoxide detector if there's gas or a fireplace?**
   _Answer:_ yes, working  ·  some missing/old  ·  none

▶ **Any cracked panes, windows that won't open/lock, or broken seals (foggy glass)?**
   _Answer:_ all fine  ·  minor  ·  cracked/broken/won't operate

▶ **Inside, is there heavy odor (smoke/pet), staining, or damage to walls/ceilings?**
   _Answer:_ clean  ·  minor wear  ·  heavy odor or staining

▶ **Is the flooring worn, damaged, or dated enough that you'd expect a buyer to want it redone?**
   _Answer:_ good  ·  some wear  ·  worn/damaged/dated

▶ **Is the kitchen dated or worn (cabinets, counters, fixtures)? This is your judgment, not a defect.**
   _Answer:_ updated  ·  somewhat dated  ·  very dated

▶ **Kitchen: are the cabinet pulls, faucet, and light fixtures dated or builder-basic? (Cheap to refresh, often high return.)**
   _Answer:_ modern/updated  ·  basic but fine  ·  dated/builder-grade

▶ **Throughout the home, are light fixtures dated (brass, bowl lights, etc.)?**
   _Answer:_ updated  ·  mixed  ·  mostly dated

▶ **Are interior wall colors dated or bold enough that neutral paint would help it show better?**
   _Answer:_ neutral/current  ·  some rooms  ·  dated/bold throughout

▶ **Are interior door handles and cabinet/door hardware dated or mismatched?**
   _Answer:_ updated  ·  fine  ·  dated/mismatched

▶ **Curb appeal: would the entry, paint, or landscaping benefit from a low-cost refresh (front door color, mulch, pressure wash)?**
   _Answer:_ already strong  ·  could use a little  ·  needs work

## What changed from the flat version (for the build)

- **No "only if you have one" text.** Presence is a real gate. The child question is shown or not shown; the prompt never hedges.

- **Fireplace is the model:** "Is there a fireplace?" → only if Yes do the fireplace condition questions appear. Same pattern for deck, garage, crawlspace, etc.

- **Smoke/CO detectors split from fireplace.** They are now their own always-asked question, not bundled with the fireplace.

- **Where a clean gate isn't possible, the answer set includes a real "Doesn't apply / N-A" option** — never a caveat in the question text.
